StockScanner Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

StockScanner is a personal stock market alert system that sends SMS notifications to the owner/operator only.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected from third parties
  • Phone numbers are used solely for delivering alerts to the registered owner

Data Sharing

  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
  • SMS messages are sent via Twilio secure infrastructure

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe from SMS alerts.

Contact

For questions, contact the application owner.
